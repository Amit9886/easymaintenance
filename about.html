Easy Maintenance

Easy Maintenance tool provides you an easy way to track and calculate your apartment maintenance details. Just few clicks can do the calculation of expenditure per flat. Follow the below steps and you are done.

  • Fill up an excel sheet with total expense for each section like water, security, electricity etc and save it.
  • Login to Easy Maintenance tool with your registered user name and password.
  • Click on Generate Report.
  • Upload the excel sheet. This will display the total expenditure of each section in a table.
  • Select last column to denote the distribution (like Equal/Sq. Ft wise) for each section.
  • Click on Generate Report button. It will display the tabular view of the cost per flat.
  • Click on Download button to download the report in an excel sheet.
